 About College

NIT Patna is the one of oldest institute in our country infact 3rd in India after IITR and College of Engineering Pune . In 2004 ,it was upgraded into NIT by MHRD govt.of India by NIT act. Before it was known as Bihar College of Engineering(BCE). Civil engineering of NIT Patna is in top position in India and infact 2 rank after IIT R. NIT Patna is also famous for water resource and technology. Nit Patna also had served in second world war by making weapons. Most of the machines in the workshop were made in Britain....National Institute of Technology Patna is the 18th National Institute of Technology created by the Ministry of H.R.D. Government of India after rechristening the erstwhile Bihar College of Engineering Patna on 28. 01. 2004. NIT Patna marked its humble beginning in 1886 with the establishment of pleaders survey training school which was subsequently promoted of Bihar College of Engineering Patna in 1924. This made this institute the 6th Oldest Engineering Institute of India. The graduate level curriculum was later elevated to the post graduate level in 1978.NIT Patna is situated on the South bank of river Ganges behind Gandhi Ghat, one of the most important and sacred places of Patna . The Institute campus is 8 Km from the Patna junction railway station and 20 Km from the Jai Prakash Narayan International Airport, Patna . The entrance to the institute is from Ashok Rajpath and it is about 3Km from the famous Gandhi Maidan. The Institute is residential in nature and provides residential facilities to its students as well as teachers as per availability. Its 60 acres campus has a picturesque river view with eyecatching historic buildings presenting a spectacle of architectural and natural beauty.

 Mode Of Admission

    For B.TECH:
  • One needs to take the JEE (Main) exam and either secure at least 75% marks (65% for SC/ST/PwD) in Class XII or be within the category-wise top 20 percentile in his/her respective Class XII board examination.
  • Admissions are done on the basis of rank in JEE (Main) through JoSAA Counselling and CSAB Special Round.
    • ... For M.TECH:
  • Eligibility criteria to M.Tech./M.Arch. admission will be 6.5 CGPA (on a 10-point scale) or 60% for OC/OB whereas 6.0 CGPA (on a 10-point scale) or 55% in case of SC/ST/PWD candidates in the qualifying degree.
  • Admissions are based on merit on the basis of the scores achieved in GATE.
  • Online Registration and Counselling for NIT Patna is done by CCMT.

    Corona & Melange

    Corona & Melange The annual technical fest of NIT Patna has become one of the biggest technical fest of the country. The last edition attracted participants from over 150 institutes from across the country, thus making it the largest technical fest of Bihar and Jharkhand.The fact there is lot more to engineering than just formulae and theories is being forgotten in the country . To keep to the long history and heritage of NIT Patna Corona 16 the annual techno management fest is all geared up to take it to the next level. Corona is the spirit that shows the budding engineers new path , a path that will utilize indigenous thinking and creativity for the benefit of the nation and the human race it self and moulds them as the managers of the future.There are many Technical events for every department , many fun events and many cultural events to bring the best out of every student.
